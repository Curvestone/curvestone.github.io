Who we are

Eduvise has been created by Simply Learning Tuition, an education consultancy based in London. Our mission is to simplify every step of the academic and career pathway, allowing students to fulfil their academic potential and enjoy a rewarding and successful life.

For more than a decade, our consultants have been trusted by parents to advise on every step of their children’s academic journey. This advice has helped to remove stress, navigate complexity and in many cases, turn students’ dreams into reality. 

We believe that by harnessing technology, we can democratise this information, providing everyone with the same independent and impartial advice to help them make the right decisions about their education and their career. Eduvise will ultimately help many more students to fulfil their true potential, empowering them to get better grades and better jobs.
