ESG Integration

  • Analysis of investment and operations to determine relevant ESG requirements.
  • Design and integrate customised ESG frameworks, including relevant policies, processes and procedures.
  • Design and integrate ESG research frameworks, including ESG data and bespoke research.
  • Design and integrate customised Monitoring & Evaluation models.
  • Expert Counsel and Committee representation.
  • Support internal teams, such as Investor Relations, with ESG requirements.
  • Deliver tailored ESG Training & Development.
  • Internal Corporate Responsibility and Culture footprinting.

  1. Enhance investee analysis, through the integration of quantitative and qualitative ESG insights into investment processes.

  4. Compliance against client mandates, as well as any regulatory considerations.
  5. Meet the growing demands from investors, investment consultants and civil society.
