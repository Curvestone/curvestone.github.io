About us

At Kukua, we provide specialist RI, ESG and Impact advisory and consulting services to the asset management community, enabling them to navigate what can be a complex space simply.

Kukua’s background is rich in the capital markets, international politics and economic ecosystems. We understand the interaction and impact between all and mobilise all our knowledge and resources to deliver value to our clients.

Our principal themes are to be curious and challenge the status quo; be though influences looking at the bigger picture; and question assumptions to enable and accelerate our client’s ability to solve their challenges.

Why Kukua?

We have an unparalleled and demonstrable track record supporting clients in this space.
We are agile and bridge the complexity, knowledge and implementation gap, enabling our clients to not only meet their RI, ESG, Impact and SDG requirements, but importantly also identify where competitive advantages can be yielded.
We bring a diverse skill and knowledge base and our work is centred on a progressive philosophy. It is this distinct thinking that enables us to accelerate the solving of complex challenges enabling our clients to reach new frontiers.
We minimise our clients’ internal resourcing, whilst ensuring regular engagement with them.
We have a deep understanding of the growing needs of the asset owner, shareholder and investment consultant community.
We are independent to the investment and operational team, which allows us to be neutral in our work.

  • Expert Counsel and Committee representation.
